Introduction

Seva Ads brings interactive digital outdoor advertisements inside public vehicles and public spaces. We want to disrupt the outdoor advertisement market, currently, digital advertisement is made for only big players who can afford a marketing cost of millions. Thus, small or medium size business needs to choose an option like banners and posters which is not effective and create heavy wastage. Seva Ads brings the solution more cheaply by bringing the ads closer and in front of the users, it's harder for users to skip our advertisements compared to social networking advertisements.
